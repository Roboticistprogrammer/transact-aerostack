A warehouse management system should offer functionalities that make daily operations smooth.
Inventory and Stock Control

    Barcode scanner integration for quick item identification
    Cloud-based storage system to update stock levels
    Automated alerts for low inventory

According to Supply Chain Dive, barcode scanning reduces warehouse errors by 41%, significantly improving order accuracy and stock control.
Order Management

    Ordering module for tracking inbound and outbound shipments
    Retail and e-commerce integration with platforms like SAP, Oracle EBS, and Odoo
    Return management with automated refund processing

Warehouse Layout and Yard Management

    Digital maps of storage areas and yard sections
    Efficient stock placement and retrieval tracking

Reporting and Analytics

    Database-powered insights for better forecasting
    Unicommerce and Microsoft PowerApps support for detailed analytics
    Performance metrics for improved decision-making
